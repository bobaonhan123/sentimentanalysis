Labeling Strategy — Weak Supervision

User-assigned star ratings are noisy — a reviewer may give 4★ but write an overwhelmingly negative review. src/training/labeling.py

Base Labels (from star rating)
1–2★ → Negative
3★ → Neutral
4–5★ → Positive
Override Rules (signal-based flip)
  1. Keyword signal: Count pos/neg keywords across pros + cons + advice
  2. ABSA signal: Score aspect opinion terms; cons column weighted 1.5× (complaints are more detailed)
  3. Combined override:
    • Neutral + signal ≤ −1.5 → flip Negative
    • Neutral + signal ≥ +1.5 → flip Positive
    • Positive + ABSA ≤ −3 → flip Negative
