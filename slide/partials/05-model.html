Why This Is Hard
  • Neutral can mean "average", "mixed", "uncertain", or "low emotional intensity".
  • Workplace reviews often mention both pros and cons in the same document.
  • Rating labels are noisy: 4-star reviews may still contain serious complaints.

Therefore, the experiment focuses on label quality and neutral handling, not only model architecture.
