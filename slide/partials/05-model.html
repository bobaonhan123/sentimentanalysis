Why This Is Hard
  • Neutral can mean "average", "mixed", "uncertain", or "low emotional intensity".
  • Workplace reviews often mention both pros and cons in the same document.
  • Rating labels are noisy: 4-star reviews may still contain serious complaints.

Therefore, the experiment focuses on label quality and neutral handling, not only model architecture.

Label Quality Audit with Confident Learning

Method
  1. Train out-of-fold probabilistic models on the full dataset.
  2. Estimate which labels are unlikely given the model confidence.
  3. Export suspicious rows for audit and train a cleaned 3-class version.
Output

The audit file keeps the ranked suspicious labels for manual review.
