Practical Value of the Topic

For Business Analysis

Converts unstructured employee reviews into measurable sentiment, aspect distribution, and time trends.

For Market Listening

Helps compare companies and industries by public employee feedback without reading thousands of reviews.

For Decision Support

Highlights critical topics such as salary, work environment, workload, and facilities as early warning signals.

The topic is valuable because it turns scattered opinions into structured business signals.

Summary

What We Built
  • Self-collected 10,000-review Vietnamese workplace dataset
  • End-to-end crawl, preprocess, label, train, analyze, and dashboard pipeline
  • Label quality audit using confident learning
  • Multiple neutral-handling strategies tested empirically
  • ABSA over 10 workplace aspects with 32,789 mentions
