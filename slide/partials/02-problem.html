Job - Pain - Gain

Job

HR and managers need to understand what employees complain about, where it happens, and how urgent it is.

Pain

Reviews are unstructured, Vietnamese text is noisy, and star ratings hide mixed opinions.

Gain

The dashboard turns raw reviews into sentiment trends, critical aspects, and action priorities.

Reviews go in -> HR priorities come out.

Project Goals

1
Build the data pipeline
Collect, clean, and structure Vietnamese workplace reviews.
2
Train sentiment models
Compare ML, embedding-based, and deep learning models under noisy labels.
3
Extract business insights
Identify critical issues by aspect, industry, company group, and time.
4
Deliver a dashboard
Make the trained model and ABSA analysis usable for non-technical users.
