Why Sentiment Analysis on Reviews?

The Gap

A 4★ rating may still hide serious complaints about salary or career growth. Star ratings alone are insufficient to guide HR decisions.

Vietnamese NLP Challenge

Standard tools (VADER, TextBlob) are English-only. Vietnamese has limited labeled corpora and requires dedicated word segmentation.

Business Motivation

Banks & fintech competing for talent need aspect-level insights — not aggregated scores — to act on specific pain points.

Research Objectives
  1. Build a 3-class sentiment classifier for Vietnamese workplace reviews
  2. Apply ABSA to identify which aspects drive satisfaction
  3. Design a complete pipeline — crawl → label → train → visualize
