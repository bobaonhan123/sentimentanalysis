Job - Pain - Gain

Job

Business teams need to understand what employees complain about, where it happens, and how urgent it is.

Pain

Reviews are unstructured, Vietnamese text is noisy, and star ratings hide mixed opinions.

Gain

The dashboard turns raw reviews into sentiment trends, critical aspects, and action priorities.

Reviews go in -> business priorities come out.

Flow Architecture

Review Dataset

Vietnamese workplace reviews with text, rating, company, industry, time, and profile metadata.

Data Preprocessing

Clean text, normalize Vietnamese, tokenize, create weak labels, and audit noisy labels.

Text Representation

Convert text into features: TF-IDF Word+Char, FastText vectors, sequence tokens, or transformer inputs.

Train & Compare Models

Evaluate multiple settings: original 3-class, cleaned 3-class, binary, mixed-conflict, and ABSA rules.

Business Outputs

Sentiment predictions, aspect-level insights, trend monitoring, segment hotspots, and dashboard views.
