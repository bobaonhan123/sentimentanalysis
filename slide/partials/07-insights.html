Business Recommendations from Aspect Analysis

Recommendations use four triggers: negative volume, negative ratio, year-over-year movement, and company-level concentration.

P1 - Compensation Benchmark

Evidence: Salary & Benefits has 5,626 negative mentions and reaches 77.5% negative in Software IT 2025.

Action: review salary bands, bonus timing, allowances, and benefit communication against competitors.

P1 - Team Climate Review

Evidence: Work Environment has 5,087 negative mentions and rose +18.1pp in Software IT.

Action: run team-level pulse checks, inspect workload and manager feedback, then prioritize high-risk teams.

P2 - Segment Watchlist

Evidence: BMBSOFT shows 85% negative for Salary and Work Environment, unlike larger positive cases.

Action: triage by industry, company, year, and aspect before applying a generic HR intervention.

P2 - Early Warning Rule

Evidence: static averages hide year-specific spikes such as Software IT in 2025.

Action: trigger review when an aspect rises >10pp, exceeds 50% negative, or crosses 100 recent mentions.
