Aspect Negativity Trend - Last 36 Months

Chart Takeaways
  • Facilities & Tools increased from 19.4% to 36.7% negative across the compared windows.
  • Work Environment also worsened, rising from 17.9% to 34.0% negative.
  • The latest monthly hotspot is Facilities & Tools in 2026-04: 33.3% negative from 33 mentions.
Interpretation

The trend is not just static dissatisfaction: recent reviews show rising operational friction around tools, facilities, and work environment quality.

Key Findings

1
Salary & Benefits is the main pain point (5,626 negative mentions, 60% negative)

Salary is the only high-volume aspect where negative sentiment clearly exceeds positive sentiment.

2
Work Environment is polarizing (10,933 total mentions)

It is the most praised aspect, but also the second largest complaint bucket; experience likely differs by team/company.

3
Time comparison shows 2025 as the stress period

Salary negativity reaches 74.1% in 2025, while Work Environment rises to 62.1%; both cool down in 2026 YTD.

4
Representative companies make the insight actionable

FPT points to work-life balance, LGEDV to technology/product, Techcombank to salary, and DISION TECH to workload pressure.
