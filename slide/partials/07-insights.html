Opinion Term Extraction (OTE)

  2. Scan ±3 token window for opinion terms
  3. Match against lexicons: 24 positive / 32 negative terms
  4. Fallback: infer from source field (pros → positive, cons → negative)

Key Findings

Work-Life Balance — 83% negative (81 pos vs 383 neg)

Dominated by: overtime, áp lực, quá tải, stress, deadline. Employees are burning out.

🔴
Career Growth — 80% negative (348 pos vs 1,423 neg)

Limited promotion tracks, insufficient training budget. Strongest pain point by volume.

🟡
Work Environment — Most discussed (3,378 mentions · 54% negative)

Both the most praised (1,568) and most complained about (1,810). Culture is polarizing — varies by team.

🟡
Management — 60% negative (293 pos vs 434 neg)

Keywords: thiếu tầm nhìn, không công bằng, micro-manage. Leadership quality inconsistent.

🟢
Salary & Benefits — Most balanced (1,617 pos vs 1,397 neg · 46% neg)
